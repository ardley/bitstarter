PushOut is a way to push a message quickly across all your devices. It views what you've copied onto your system clipboard, and gives you an option next to the 'paste' option to instead send it to all your devices as a push notification. When it arrives later that second, you're just one tap or click away from having it ready to paste anywhere in that device. PushOut enables you to switch work seamlessly across devices, while saving you at least 15 seconds and 15 clicks, swipes and drags vs. pasting a link in Evernote.

PushOut is a free service, that also saves the content of your pushouts so you can access old pushed messages, links, and text easily and searchably online, where you'll also see ads that will pay for keeping the service free. If you never look up old pushouts, you'll never even see an ad. The purpose of this crowdfunding effort is to finance development of an app for iOS, Android, OSX, and Windows, as well as Chrome and Firefox extensions to upload and push it across those devices with a single tap/click. When the service has users and traction, we'll work on getting it baked into operating systems directly, so instead of rightclicking in your browser and selecting a menu option, you can use a simple keystroke such as Ctrl+Alt+P, or get an option next to "Select All" and "Copy" when you longpress in your phone.

FAQ

Is it up and running now?
Not just yet. Check back often.
Will it work on my Phone/Tablet/Laptop/Desktop?
Eventually. For now it doesn't work on any of them, and it really needs at least two in order to be useful. Here's the order in which we plan to make it available: 1. As a Chrome extension. 2. As a firefox extension. 3. As an Android App and a FirefoxOS app. 4. As an iPhone & iPad app. 5. As an OSX App. 6. As a Windows app. 7. For Windows mobile (we will cooperate to facilitate the Linux desktop OS and mobile apps that will be up by this point) 8. As a standard menu option within iOS, Android, OSX, and Windows.
How will my information be used?!?!
The content of each PushOut will be transmitted to every device signed up to that account, and saved with encryption to your account history, so that AND ONLY SO THAT you can access it later if you want to. If you signed up with another account, advertising information from that account may be used to serve you more relevant advertising while you're going through the history, but your PushOuts will not me mined at all.
How do I know my PushOuts won't be intercepted by police holding radar guns?
Each time you add a device, it generates an asymmetric encryption key pair, and sends the public key to our server. Our server does the same making a key for the device. Using this secure communication channel, the server sends a uniquie authentication code to the device, so the server can verify each message the device sends, and send the message out to every device that's currently listening (connected to the internet). The encryption is built on the difficulty of factoring very large numbers that are a product of two primes (it's really no easier for supercomputers than for you).
